Early Life to Now

Raymond Harvey grew up in Riverside, California. He was raised by his grandparents, Milton & Lois Harvey. He became interested in computers at a very young age, and started teaching himself how to program them. Though his grandmother assumed his interest in computers would be like any other interest of a boy that age and forgotten about in a month, his grandfather somehow knew this was different and helped Raymond get his first computer. On top of having a computer at all, his grandfather helped him get other parts he needed as well when they were no longer being used at the university where he worked as a chef. Working with computers came quite naturally to Raymond. He learned and practiced everything he could about programming (in many cases by trial and error), and eventually had an idea for what would become his life's work. This is when he decided to build Adax. (see link above) But he has also done many other things. He has worked for quite a few companies. And has even owned one or two. Now, despite how long ago he started his work, he continues to learn everything he can and build more things for many people and companies.
